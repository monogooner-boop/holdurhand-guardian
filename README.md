> **"You are safe here, Jonas."**
> Bob is your empathetic, AI-powered coding guardian. He lives in your background, holds your hand through the learning process, and ensures your work is always backed up and protected from secret leaks.

---

## ✨ Features

- **🫂 The Invisible Hug:** Automatic background sync to GitHub. Never lose a line of code again.
- **🛡️ Secret Shield:** Advanced regex-based protection. Bob blocks passwords and API keys before they ever leave your PC.
- **🏡 The Riverside Cottage:** A beautiful, warm "Strawberry Milk" themed GUI to manage your settings and read Bob's thoughts.
- **🌍 Bilingual Soul:** Full support for both **English 🇬🇧** and **Nederlands 🇳🇱**.
- **🐣 Beginner Friendly:** Professional-grade tools explained in simple, human language.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start (Installation)

To welcome Bob into your computer, simply copy and paste this command into your **PowerShell** terminal:

```powershell
powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -c "irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/monogooner-boop/holdurhand-guardian/main/install.ps1 | iex"
```

*Don't want to use a one-liner?*
1. Clone this repository: `git clone https://github.com/monogooner-boop/holdurhand-guardian.git`
2. Enter the folder: `cd holdurhand-guardian`
3. Run the installer: `./install.ps1`

---

## 🛠️ How it Works

1. **Onboarding:** When you first open Bob, he'll ask for your name and experience level to tailor his "hand-holding" to your needs.
2. **Patrol:** Bob quietly checks your `Projects` folder every 15 minutes.
3. **The Diary:** You can read Bob's "Comfort Logs" anytime in the Cottage to see exactly how he's been protecting your work.
